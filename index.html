Tampines Secondary School End-of-Year Examination 2023 Paper 4 Oral

READ THESE INSTRUCTIONS FIRST

The oral test will consist of the following two parts:

Part 1 – Planned Response

You will watch a short video clip and deliver a planned response of up to 2 minutes to a given prompt.

Part 2 – Spoken Interaction

You will engage in a discussion with the Examiners on a topic broadly related to the video clip.

You have 10 minutes of preparation time to watch the video clip and plan your response.
