Video Preparation Task

10:00

You will watch a video which shows people at a restaurant buffet.

You will deliver a planned response of up to 2 minutes to the following prompt:

Do you think activities like the one shown in the video are a good way for people to interact? Why do you say so?

You can make notes on the paper provided and refer to them during the examination.
Your notes will not be assessed.
At the end of the examination, the notes must be handed in to the Examiners.
